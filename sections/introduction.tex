\section{Goal, Research Questions and Approach}
\label{sec:intro-goal-rq-approach}

From the observations in previous sections \ldots

In this thesis we aim to contribute to the transparency of the Internet, and thus we define the following goal:

\begin{quote}
    \itshape \textbf{Goal:} to increase transparency of the Internet by empirically evaluating different network types, revealing common services deployment patterns on anycast, shedding light on the unexplored \acrshort{ldap} ecosystem and, disclose use of national level \acrshort{dmarc} usage.
\end{quote}

\begin{quote}
    \itshape \textbf{RQ 1:} How can we increase transparency on anycast networks?
\end{quote}

\begin{quote}
    \itshape \textbf{RQ 2:} Can we increase transparency by shedding light on the unexplored \acrshort{ldap} ecosystem and its \gls{tls} practices?
\end{quote}

\begin{quote}
    \itshape \textbf{RQ 3:} How can we increase transparency in national and governmental domains that use \acrshort{dmarc}?
\end{quote}
